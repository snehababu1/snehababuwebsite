Shaped by Many Worlds

Hi, I'm someone who doesn't just work across industries—I live across them. With a BA in Media, Communication, and Business Psychology from Bangalore University and an MBA in International Business from Birmingham City University, my career has been shaped by a love for understanding what makes people tick. My journey has taken me from Oman and India to the UK and Germany, turning me into a cultural chameleon who can adapt to just about any environment.

Professionally, I've spent over three years blending the creative world of marketing (think SEO, social media, Google Ads, and content creation) with the structured world of project management and operations. But I don't stop there. I have a massive curiosity for how things move and connect, which draws me toward the tech, IT, and logistics sectors too.

At my core, I am a listener first and a strategist second. My cross-cultural adventures have made me deeply empathetic, highly adaptive, and a bit of a perfectionist when it comes to the details. I'm also a big believer in working smarter, regularly teaming up with AI tools to supercharge my creativity and keep projects running flawlessly. Whether I'm designing an email campaign, mapping out operational workflows, or mastering my next German conversation (C1 is in the works!), I bring a global perspective and a lot of heart to everything I do.
